# Particles

Particles are short markers that show what role each word plays in a sentence. English uses word order and prepositions for this; Japanese tags nouns directly with particles. Learn these and half the grammar fog lifts.

Brief summaries live here; full usage and contrast pages are under `grammar/`.

## wa (は)

**は** — **topic marker**. "As for X…"
Marks what the sentence is *about*. Pronounced `wa` even though it's written with the `ha` kana — historical spelling.
- 私は学生です (watashi wa gakusei desu) = "I am a student."

See [grammar/particle-wa-vs-ga.md](../grammar/particle-wa-vs-ga.md).

## ga (が)

**が** — **subject marker**. Identifies *who does* the verb, or highlights new information.
- 猫がいます (neko ga imasu) = "There's a cat (new information)."
- Contrast: 猫はかわいいです (neko wa kawaii desu) = "(Speaking of) cats, they're cute."

## wo (を)

**を** — **direct object marker**. Pronounced simply `o` in modern Japanese.
- パンを食べます (pan wo tabemasu) = "I eat bread."

See [grammar/particle-wo.md](../grammar/particle-wo.md).

## ni (に)

**に** — **direction / destination / time / indirect object / location of existence**.
Swiss-army particle. Main uses:
- 学校に行きます (gakkou ni ikimasu) = "I go to school." (destination)
- 七時に起きます (shichi-ji ni okimasu) = "I wake up at 7." (time)
- 友達に会います (tomodachi ni aimasu) = "I meet a friend." (indirect object)
- 駅にあります (eki ni arimasu) = "It's at the station." (location with ある/いる)

See [grammar/particle-ni.md](../grammar/particle-ni.md).

## de (で)

**で** — **location where an action happens / means by which it's done**.
- 家で食べます (ie de tabemasu) = "I eat at home."
- 電車で行きます (densha de ikimasu) = "I go by train."

Contrast with に: `に` marks static location of existence; `で` marks where an action is performed. See [grammar/particle-de.md](../grammar/particle-de.md).

## no (の)

**の** — **possessive / modifying**. Essentially a genitive-plus: "X's Y" or "Y of X."
- 私の本 (watashi no hon) = "my book."
- 日本語の先生 (nihongo no sensei) = "teacher of Japanese."

See [grammar/particle-no.md](../grammar/particle-no.md).

## to (と)

**と** — "and" (exhaustive list of nouns) / "with (someone)."
- パンと水 (pan to mizu) = "bread and water."
- 友達と行きます (tomodachi to ikimasu) = "I go with a friend."

## mo (も)

**も** — "also / too."
Replaces は or が when the meaning is "X too" or "also X."
- 私も学生です (watashi mo gakusei desu) = "I too am a student."

## kara (から)

**から** — "from" (starting point) / "because" (when following a clause).
- 東京から来ました (toukyou kara kimashita) = "I came from Tokyo."

## made (まで)

**まで** — "until / as far as" (endpoint).
- 駅まで歩きます (eki made arukimasu) = "I walk as far as the station."
