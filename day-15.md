# Day 15 — わかる, 買う, and に for Direction

**Target time:** 15–20 minutes.

## 1 · New vocabulary (2)

| Kanji | Kana | Romaji | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| わかる | わかる | wakaru | to understand / know |
| 買う | かう | kau | to buy |

Polite: わかります (wakarimasu), 買います (kaimasu).

⚠️ **わかる takes が, not を.** Say 日本語**が**わかります — "I understand Japanese." Some English-transitive verbs are intransitive in Japanese. See [vocab/essential-verbs.md#wakaru](./vocab/essential-verbs.md#wakaru).

## 3 · Grammar concept: に for direction / destination

You met に for time on day 13 — today it also means "to (a place)." With motion verbs (行く, 来る, 帰る "return"), the destination takes に.

```
[place] に [motion verb]
```

- 学校に行きます。 — "I go to school."
- 家に帰ります。 — "I return home." (kaeru = return)
- 日本に来ます。 — "(Someone) will come to Japan."

You can also use へ (pronounced `e`) for direction, with a slightly more formal / "toward" feeling. に is more common in everyday speech.

Full page: [grammar/particle-ni.md](./grammar/particle-ni.md).

## 4 · Reading practice

Read aloud and translate:

1. 私は日本語がわかります。
2. すみません、わかりません。
3. 明日、パンを買います。
4. 店に行きます。 (mise = shop — you'll meet it on day 21)

<details><summary>Translations</summary>

1. "I understand Japanese."
2. "Sorry, I don't understand." (A useful escape hatch.)
3. "Tomorrow, I'll buy bread."
4. "I'll go to the shop."

</details>

## 5 · Speaking practice

Try these aloud:
- 英語がわかりますか？ — "Do you understand English?"
  - Reply: はい、わかります。 / いいえ、わかりません。
- 明日、何を買いますか？
  - Reply: [item] を買います。

Bonus stock phrase to remember: **ちょっと待ってください** (chotto matte kudasai — "please wait a moment"). You're not meant to conjugate this yet; just memorize the shape.

## 6 · Quiz

1. Which particle does わかる take for what's understood?
2. Translate: 明日、日本に行きます。
3. Translate: 日本語がわかりません。
4. What's the polite form of 買う?
5. Fill in: 学校 ___ 行きます。

<details><summary>Answers</summary>

1. が (not を).
2. "Tomorrow, I'll go to Japan."
3. "I don't understand Japanese."
4. 買います (kaimasu)
5. に (destination)

</details>
